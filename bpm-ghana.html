Brought to you as part of The Bridge by B.P.M.

Bridging culture and creativity across the diaspora.

Overview

As we increase our global impact, we are proud to maintain amazing partnerships in Accra, Ghana! B.P.M. continues to provide immersive, exciting and practical training in music production through hands-on workshops, masterclasses and performance/ recording opportunities for students age 10+ (adults too).

Goals

  • Expand access to music technology education
  • Promote cultural exchange and innovation
  • Develop career awareness and skills
  • Empower creative expression
